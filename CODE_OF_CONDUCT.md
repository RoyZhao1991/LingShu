# Code of Conduct / 社区行为准则

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in LingShu a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

我们致力于建设一个尊重事实、尊重劳动、允许不同观点并且没有骚扰的协作社区。

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Share reproducible evidence when making technical claims.
- Respect privacy, credentials, unpublished work, and security boundaries.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when needed, and repair harm.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials without explicit permission.
- Deliberately misleading demonstrations, fabricated test results, or concealed security risks.
- Sustained disruption, spam, or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this code, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. For a confidential report, use [GitHub private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/RoyZhao1991/LingShu/security/advisories/new) and mark the report as a conduct concern.

Enforcement decisions should consider context, impact, repair, and community safety. Retaliation against a good-faith reporter is not permitted.

## Scope

This code applies in repository spaces and when a person represents the LingShu project in public spaces.

This policy is inspired by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), version 2.1.
